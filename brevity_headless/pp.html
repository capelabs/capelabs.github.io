Privacy Policy - Brevity

Effective Date: June 1, 2025
Last Updated: June 12, 2025

CapeLabs Research Group ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you subscribe to and use our newsletter service, Brevity.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information:

  • Email Address – Required to deliver the Brevity newsletter and related communications.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To send you the Brevity newsletter.
  • To communicate updates and announcements.
  • For internal analytics and service improvement.
  • For direct marketing related to CapeLabs Research Group's activities (you may opt out at any time).

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Consent: You have given your consent by subscribing to our newsletter.
  • Legitimate Interests: For purposes such as marketing and analytics that are not overridden by your data protection rights.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share your data with the following third-party service provider:

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) – Provides infrastructure and email delivery. AWS is contractually obligated to handle your data securely and in compliance with applicable laws.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your email address only for as long as you are subscribed to the Brevity newsletter.

You may unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email or by contacting us directly.

Upon unsubscribing, your email address and associated data will be promptly and permanently deleted from our system.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

Under GDPR:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to data portability

Under CCPA (California Residents):

  • Right to know what personal data we collect
  • Right to request deletion of personal data
  • Right to opt out of data selling (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@thecapelabs.com.

7. Data Transfers

We may transfer your personal information to countries outside of your jurisdiction (including the United States), where data protection laws may differ. When we do so, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this policy.

8. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the service after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:
